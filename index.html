ABOUT US

Fixora Trading and Services, founded in 2025, is a dynamic Qatar-based company offering a powerful combination of wholesale trading and professional services. Built on nearly a decade of industry experience, Fixora specializes in importing high-demand products from Dubai, China & Bangladesh—such as electronics, gadgets, cosmetics, watches, perfumes, garments, and more—for wholesale distribution in Qatar.

In addition, Fixora supplies a wide range of essential goods including construction materials, industrial tools, PPE, Pantry, Disposable Items, Cleaning items, office and school supplies and auto spare parts to support ongoing projects and businesses. We also offer reliable services like AC maintenance, manpower support, cleaning and general installations. With a strong supplier network, deep market knowledge, and a customer-first approach, Fixora is committed to becoming a trusted name in both trading and service sectors across Qatar.
