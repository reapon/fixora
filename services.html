We specialize in the trading and timely supply of essential materials and products across various sectors. Whether you're managing a construction site, a retail outlet, an office, or a hospitality business, Fixora ensures reliable delivery and competitive pricing.

Construction & Industrial Materials

  • Building Materials: Basic Consumables, binding wires, tie wires, nails, screws, sealants, adhesives, tapes, drill bits, paint tools, spray foam, Polythene sheets, tarpaulins, basic hardware like door stoppers and hinges etc.
  • Industrial Tools & Equipment: Hand tools, power tools, ladders, drills, cutting machines, etc.
  • Auto Spare Parts & Accessories: Filters, brake pads, wipers, lights, lubricants, and general accessories etc.

Safety & Protection Gear

  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Helmets, gloves, safety vests, goggles, coveralls, safety shoes, ear protection & other site safety essentials.
  • Ideal for construction sites, maintenance teams, and industrial zones.

Office & School Supplies

  • Stationery: Papers, pens, files, folders, markers, staplers, notebooks, envelopes, calculators, clips, toners, and other daily-use stationery items.
  • School Items: Exercise books, art supplies, whiteboards, markers, student kits, etc.

Cleaning, Pantry & Disposable Supplies

  • Cleaning Items: Detergents, floor cleaners, brooms, mops, disinfectants, air fresheners, etc.
  • Pantry Supplies: Tea, coffee, sugar, milk powder, bottled water, paper cups, etc.
  • Disposable Products: Tissue rolls, garbage bags, gloves, paper towels, disposable cutlery, etc.

Lifestyle & Retail Products

  • Perfumes & Cosmetics: Body sprays, perfumes, creams, shampoos, lotions, personal care items, etc.
  • Toys & Gifts: Kids’ toys, return gifts, decorative items for shops and events.
  • Electronics & Gadgets: Mobile accessories, smartwatches, headphones, power banks, small electronic devices, etc.
